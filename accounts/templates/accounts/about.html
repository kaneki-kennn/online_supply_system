ABOUT THE SYSTEM

Welcome to Cebu Technological University-Argao Campus Online System, your dedicated solution for optimizing procurement and supply management. We understand the unique challenges universities face when it comes to sourcing, acquiring, and distributing educational supplies. Our online supply system is here to simplify these processes and enhance operational efficiency for the institution.

Key Features:

  • Efficient Procurement: Simplify the procurement process with easy access to a comprehensive network of trusted suppliers, allowing you to source educational materials and supplies seamlessly.
  • Real-time Tracking: Gain real-time visibility into your supply chain, ensuring that textbooks, classroom equipment, and other essential supplies are delivered to your campus on time.
  • Customizable Solutions: Tailor University Supply to meet the unique needs of your institution with customized modules and integrations.
  • Responsive Support: Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to provide assistance and guidance, ensuring a smooth user experience.
